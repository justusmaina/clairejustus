The creeva foundation is one develoed with the purpose of assisting the homeless children on the streets to find a good education via sponsors and well wishers. it aims at reducing the numer of homeless children and in the long run a better nation. We assist any child who is on the streets of our beautiful town without anyone to look after them.

Sponsorship

We find each childs ability and from there on we register him or her in the organization system. From there we look for a sponsor who is interested in that field of the childs ability and woe them to sponsor the child. The advantage spools over to the sponsor and the child. The child then goes through training and when ready is released to be under the watch of himself or herself.

Children

The children that are in most cases picked are within the age of 0-14years of age. The young ones(0-5) are taken care of at the center and are still under well wishers. The older ones are released to the well wisher and the well wisher has the choice to choose whether the child will stay with them or stay at the center and be under sponsorship

costs

The costs are according to the training the child needs. Also there are costs for food and mentorship that is done at the center.
